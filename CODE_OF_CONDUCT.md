# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of background, identity, experience, or viewpoint.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful, specific, and evidence-oriented.
- Critique claims and sources, not people.
- Declare conflicts of interest when proposing your own project or a commercial service.
- Correct overclaims openly and preserve a useful audit trail.
- Respect privacy, copyright, licenses, trademarks, and responsible disclosure.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, threats, discrimination, sexualized attention, doxxing, deliberate misinformation, spam, undisclosed promotional manipulation, and repeated refusal to follow evidence rules are unacceptable.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the repository maintainers through the contact method configured on the GitHub repository. Maintainers may edit, hide, reject, or remove contributions and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Enforcement decisions should be proportionate, documented privately, and protective of reporters.

This policy is adapted from the Contributor Covenant 2.1 in plain language. The full covenant is available at https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/.
